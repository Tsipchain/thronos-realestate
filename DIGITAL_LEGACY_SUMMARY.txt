                            KEY INNOVATION
================================================================================

Problem:
  - Bitcoin: If owner dies, billions in assets are lost forever (no heir access)
  - Ethereum: No mechanism for legal inheritance (private keys cannot be shared)
  - Current Solutions: Must trust lawyers, banks, or centralized services

Thronos Solution:
  - NFT-based digital wills stored immutably on blockchain
  - Biometric heir verification (fingerprint, face, iris, genetic markers)
  - Automated smart contract execution to distribute assets fairly
  - Complete blockchain audit trail proving no tampering occurred
  - Time-limited QR code recovery for heir access
  - 100% decentralized - no third-party intermediaries

Result:
  → Heirs can recover assets with cryptographic proof of legitimacy
  → Assets cannot be stolen or misused (immutable audit trail)
  → No probate delays - instant automated distribution
  → Legal-grade inheritance system (blockchain as court of law)

================================================================================
                        SECURITY ARCHITECTURE
================================================================================

THREAT: Unauthorized heir access
DEFENSE: Biometric + genetic verification (hash-based, never plaintext)
         Access token required + time expiration
         Multiple verification attempts trigger alerts

THREAT: Asset theft before distribution
DEFENSE: Immutable audit trail proves custody
         Blockchain record shows who accessed when
         Cannot retroactively claim assets were stolen

THREAT: Heir impersonation
DEFENSE: SHA256 biometric hashing (non-reversible)
         Genetic marker adds second verification layer
         Access tokens are one-time use

THREAT: Smart contract tampering
DEFENSE: Immutable contract (no upgrade path)
         Deployed once, cannot be modified
         On-chain bytecode verification

THREAT: Data leakage
DEFENSE: End-to-end encryption (AES from pledge system)
         Quantum-safe encryption via AI layer
         Private keys never exposed in audit trail
