## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**THRONOS IKE** is a technology company with physical presence and a digital Web3 ecosystem of services. The core business model has evolved to capitalize on a **unique opportunity in North Greece (Ouranoupoli & Nea Roda)** following the departure of Eldorado Gold—transforming regional real estate into a **high-yield Web3 investment platform**.

**Business Model**: 
- **Vertical 1**: Mobile app builds, blockchain infrastructure, and developer services (ThronosBuild)
- **Vertical 2**: Premium real estate portfolio management with Web3 tokenization, international investor onboarding, and digital identity verification (VerifyID + RWA Platform)
- **Vertical 3**: Health-tech monitoring, career marketplace, and digital skills training

**Strategic Differentiator**: The only integrated platform in Greece combining **blockchain-verified real estate listings**, **KYC/AML compliance via VerifyID**, **corporate legal substance in Greece**, and **smart contract-based revenue sharing** for international investors.

## 1. MARKET PROBLEM & OPPORTUNITY

### 1.1 The Regional Crisis → Global Opportunity
- **Problem**: Eldorado Gold's withdrawal left 200+ premium properties in North Greece underutilized
- **Root Cause**: International investors avoid Greek real estate due to:
  - Complex bureaucracy and slow compliance processes
  - Lack of transparent, blockchain-verified property records
  - Absence of digital identity verification (no easy KYC/AML)
  - No corporate legal structure for EU/non-EU investors
  - Low rental yield visibility and scattered management

### 1.2 Global Investor Demand
- **Web3 Community**: 15M+ crypto holders seeking alternative investments outside traditional markets
- **High-Net-Worth Individuals (HNWIs)**: Seeking EU corporate presence + tax-efficient structures
- **Tourism Growth**: Ouranoupoli/Nea Roda see 1.5M+ annual visitors (untapped short-term rental demand)
- **EU Gateway Strategy**: Greece as strategic gateway to Mount Athos tourism, Balkan expansion

### 1.3 Regulatory Tailwind
- **Greek Government**: Promoting real estate investment & startup ecosystems (Elevate Greece, Digital Transformation funds)
- **EU Regulations**: GDPR + digital identity standards create demand for verified, compliant platforms
- **Blockchain Adoption**: RWA (Real World Assets) tokenization gaining institutional traction

## 2. THE THRONOS ECOSYSTEM SOLUTION

### 2.1 Core Products & Revenue Streams

#### **Product A: VerifyID + Corporate Substance**
**What it does**:
- Instant KYC/AML verification for international investors (5 min vs. 2-4 weeks traditional)
- Blockchain-backed digital identity (no custody of official documents)
- EU-compliant corporate email, hosting, and registered office in Greece
- Smart contract-ready profiles for trading/staking on Thronoschain

**Revenue Model**:
- **Setup Fee**: €500-€1,500 per investor (one-time)
- **Monthly Substance Fee**: €50-€150 (corporate address + email + compliance audit)
- **B2B Verification API**: Subscription-based for real estate partners

---

#### **Product B: RWA Real Estate Platform**
**What it does**:
- Portfolio of 200+ premium properties in Ouranoupoli/Nea Roda
- Blockchain-verified listings + occupancy history
- Fractional ownership (tokenized shares) for properties
- Smart contract revenue splitting (rent → investor + platform + local partner)
- Direct integration with real estate partner (μεσιτικό γραφείο)

**Revenue Model**:
1. **Brokerage/Listing Fees**: 2-3% of property transaction value (partner's cut)
2. **Platform Subscription**: €300-€800/month per property (for management + smart contracts)
3. **Tokenization Minting Fee**: 1.5-2.5% of token volume minted on Thronoschain
4. **Rental Yield Sharing**: 2-4% of platform-managed rental income
5. **Premium Investor Dashboard**: €100-€250/month per investor account

---

#### **Product C: All-in-One Investor Package**
**Bundled offering combining A + B + existing services**:

1. **VerifyID + Compliance**: Complete KYC/AML + corporate structure
2. **Property Access**: Curated portfolio of 50+ premium properties
3. **Legal Entity Setup**: Support for establishing Greek LLC/ΙΚΕ with full tax footprint
4. **Smart Contract Integration**: Automated rent collection and payout to investor wallet
5. **24/7 Support**: English/Greek technical + legal support

**Pricing**: €2,500-€5,000 per investor (setup) + €250-€400/month (recurring)
