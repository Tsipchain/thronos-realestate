⚠️ IMPORTANT: Do NOT Use Exchange (MEXC, Binance, Kraken)

Direct CEX deposits are NOT allowed for security and KYC/AML compliance.
Follow these steps instead:

  1. Login to MEXC/Binance/Kraken
  2. Click "Withdraw" or "Send"
  3. Send BTC to your personal wallet (MetaMask, Ledger, Rabby, Trust Wallet, etc.)
  4. Wait for confirmation (5-30 minutes)
  5. From your personal wallet, complete the pledge below

Why? Exchanges use shared hot wallets. We can't verify your KYC that way.
Personal wallet = unique address = verified identity ✅

BTC-only pledge → THR. Other chains are bridge/balance only.
