❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Which hardware type should I choose?

Choose based on your available equipment: ASIC for maximum rewards, GPU for flexibility, CPU for accessibility, USB for education, IoT for distributed networks.

Q: How do heat bonuses work?

Submit heat metrics (temperature, recovery %) and we automatically classify your operation into a tier (5% → 40% bonus). Use case bonuses add 5-20% more.

Q: Can I update my heat metrics?

Yes! Submit new metrics anytime. We track your latest data and apply the current tier/bonus to your mining rewards.

Q: What's the difference between TIER_1 and TIER_4?

TIER_1 (5-10% recovery) = 5% bonus, TIER_2 (10-15%) = 15% bonus, TIER_3 (15-25%) = 25% bonus, TIER_4 (25%+) = 40% bonus.
