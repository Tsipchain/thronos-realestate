# Thronos Chain Whitepaper (Full)

## Core Service: Learn2Earn (L2E)

**Whitepaper wording (added):**
> Learn2Earn (L2E) is a core Thronos Chain service for wallet-native educational participation, where course economics, academic progression, and certification workflows are handled with explicit state transitions rather than opaque automation.

### Service Philosophy (Implemented)
- **THR-first pricing:** canonical course pricing is THR-native.
- **Teacher paid in THR:** instructor settlement remains THR-denominated.
- **Controlled rewards:** rewards are policy-controlled and not automatic payouts.
- **Live sessions:** courses can include live classroom/session operations.
- **Weighted quizzes and scoreboard logic:** score computation supports weighted questions and explicit pass/fail thresholds.
- **Certificate workflow:** certificate lifecycle is explicit and non-automatic.
- **Approval and issuance logic:** approvals and issuance are separate, auditable operations.
- **Tenant/university groundwork:** tenant/institution metadata and issuer identity scaffolding are integrated.
- **Wallet-native participation:** learner identity and participation operate in wallet-aligned flows.
- **Auditability and role boundaries:** RBAC boundaries and certificate audit history are represented in service operations.

### Implemented Now (Core Capability)
- pricing and enrollment model
- reward control
- live classroom support
- academic result states
- certificate lifecycle
- approval/issuance workflow
- RBAC and tenant enforcement hooks
- audit trail/history

### Future-Phase Refinement (Not Overstated as Complete)
- richer dashboards
- broader delegated governance
- stronger tenant partitioning
- advanced compliance/export/reporting

### Non-Goals in Current Representation
- No claim of fully completed governance dashboard maturity.
- No claim of full multi-tenant hard partitioning.
- No claim of automatic certificates or automatic reward issuance.
