# L2E Compliance and Exports (Phase 5)

## Compliance-safe principles
- educational access is separate from reward eligibility
- approval and issuance remain manual/controlled actions
- no guaranteed earnings and no automatic entitlement
- every critical approval/issuance action is auditable
- role accountability and tenant/institution ownership checks are enforced

## Export-ready structures
History APIs include export-ready envelopes with:
- schema version
- generated timestamp
- tenant/institution context
- actor identity/role
- reason and lifecycle transition fields
