# Learn2Earn Phase 6: Policy and Compliance

## Purpose
Phase 6 expands policy-evaluation and compliance operations on top of the stable Phases 1–5 core.

## Implemented in this phase
- internal policy evaluation structure for approval/issuance/export/visibility actions
- export-ready operational reporting payloads
- tenant and global dashboard summary endpoints
- stricter tenant/admin/delegate reporting boundaries

## Preserved guardrails
- no THR-first pricing changes
- no controlled reward issuance changes
- no automatic rewards or certificates
