# Learn2Earn Core Service Closeout

## Closeout Decision
Learn2Earn (L2E) is finalized as **completed core service infrastructure** for Thronos Chain as of April 15, 2026.

## Finalized Foundation
- Core service identity is established and documented.
- THR-first pricing remains the baseline service contract.
- Controlled reward issuance remains policy-mediated and non-automatic.
- RBAC and tenant boundaries are retained as mandatory enforcement layers.
- Certificate lifecycle remains explicit and non-automatic (request/approve/reject/issue).
- Audit history and compliance report delivery are integrated into core operations.
- Policy evaluation and provider-compatibility runtime are active with compatibility envelopes.
- Operational and observability dashboards are available for tenant and global administration.

## Closeout Guardrails (Locked)
- Do not reopen THR-first pricing.
- Do not reopen controlled reward issuance.
- Do not weaken RBAC, tenant, audit, or policy boundaries.
- Do not downgrade certificate governance to automatic issuance.

## Future Refinement Only (Post-Closeout)
These are non-blocking refinements and **not prerequisites for core-service completion**:
- deeper external policy engine execution (beyond compatibility wrappers)
- richer analytics and SLO instrumentation
- expanded delivery transport channels and retry/ack workflow hardening
- UX/reporting polish and additional operator tooling

## Closeout Status
- Core infrastructure: **complete**
- Operations handoff package: **complete**
- Launch readiness documentation: **complete**
